Features & Benefits

Custom Fundraising Terms

TransferNext uses a mechanism known as a securitized token offering (STO) to help you source funds from your investors.

Working with our team, you are able to customize any terms of the STO and associated smart contracts, including the target amount you wish to raise, the minimum (and / or maximum) investment lots, the duration of the fundraising period, the lockup time on the investment, dividend terms, etc.

Next Generation Security

TransferNext’s services are built on the back of Ethereum, the second largest blockchain network in the world with thousands of applications operating on it. Ethereum is a decentralized technology that leverages a “proof of work” (PoW) validation system to confirm all transactions that happen on its networks.

Once a transaction has been verified by the network, which is a near-instantaneous process, it becomes completely immutable. For the use case of fund transfers, this means that there is a permanent record of all transfers of capital to and from your investors, with all the terms of the investment digitally recorded, that is completely immutable. The distributed nature of the blockchain, relying on computing power and cryptic validation processes supported by computers around the world, ensures that the terms of your transactions can never be disputed, and that your funds can never go missing.

Instantaneous Fund Transfers

Traditional fund transfers generally require a multi-day settlement period in order for funds to be moved from one account to another. The need for back office professionals to move funds restricts transfers to business hours, and demands higher costs to pay for this headcount.

On a blockchain, the distributed validation of all transactions across the network by computers around the world allow for an immediate transfer of funds at any time you or one of your investors choose to execute. Integration with existing digital wallet providers will allow for funds to be moved directly in or out of bank accounts to streamline the conversion of cash to tokens on both sides of the transaction.

Greater Investor Liquidity

An advantage of using tokens to represent a given amount of investment in a fund is that they can be transferred between investors (given that they meet the legal requirements to invest in such funds). This can allow investors to liquidate their position in a fund without a redemption, ensuring that the amount of funds held remains constant over the investment horizon.

Access to Accredited Investors

Given that there are such heavy regulations on the industry with the end-goal of protecting investors, fund managers must employ services to check on their investors’ financial profile, and actively search for eligible investors when pursuing new strategies. TransferNext seeks to make it easier for alternative asset managers to source capital from potential investors by creating a community of pre-approved investors, and allow managers to communicate their fundraising needs to them.

Ease of Foreign Investment

For fund managers who seek to raise capital from a variety of investors who may be liquid in funds other than their base currency, the use of Ether as an intermediary currency for transactions can be used to quickly source funds from foreign investors without the need for potentially costly large-scale foreign exchange conversions from one currency to another. Ether is the cryptocurrency of the Ethereum blockchain network, and can be bought with most traditional sovereign currencies. If desired, Ether can be used in order to facilitate the purchase of your fund’s tokens, which can then be converted into your base currency for investments.
